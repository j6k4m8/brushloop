What BrushLoop Does

Realtime & Turn-Based

Switch between simultaneous sketching and round-robin handoffs with clear turn ownership.

Layered Workflow

Keep base photos, paint layers, and eraser edits isolated so collaborators never destroy lower layers.

Recoverable History

CRDT operation logs and snapshots let you revisit progression, not just the final canvas.

Built-In Collaboration Hub

Contacts, invites, direct chat, and artwork events live in one workspace designed for shared process.
